In the fast-paced world of car sales, managing leads efficiently can be the difference between closing a sale and missing out.

That's where our Enquiry Hub comes in—a cutting-edge web application designed to organise how dealerships handle their leads. Not only can you handle leads from multiple marketplaces, the enquiry hub allows you to handle all your lead channels in one place, through our innovative AI email inbox integration other channels is a walk in the park.



Why do you have a FREE forever plan, how does this make sense?

It is our belief that independent, smaller dealers are always left out! The bigger guys get to negotiate better pricing on marketplaces, have budgets for better technology, have access to marketing agencies, the list goes on .... we thought let's create a really cool free plan that is enough for a dealer to be able to manage all their leads in place without having to ever pay us a penny.

What restrictions come with the FREE plan?

We have tried to make our FREE plan non-restrictive so you can connect all your lead sources, marketplaces and respond to these leads via our mobile application or web application, we include 1,000 free emails per month and additional emails can be bought in packages or simply £0.01 per email.

What are the PAID features?

We have two additional optional add on products, and we are prepared to give you a trial of these add-ons should you request, to ensure they deliver a benefit to your dealership first before you pay us a penny! Firstly, we have a WhatsApp Module utilising the official WhatsApp business API you can utilise WhatsApp to communicate with customers and see a far higher response rate over email. The second add on product is our Automations module, with the creation of bots your autopilot can answer customer questions, create appointments, obtain finance applications or assist with the valuation of a part exchange to name a few features.


If you don't wish to use our WhatsApp Business API or Automations module and simply take advantage of emails, up to 1,000 responses per month will never cost you a penny, that's a promise!
