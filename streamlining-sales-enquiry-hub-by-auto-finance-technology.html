In the fast-paced world of automotive sales, the ability to quickly and efficiently manage customer inquiries can be the difference between a sale and a missed opportunity. Recognising this critical need, Auto Finance Technology introduces the Enquiry Hub, a game-changing solution designed to centralise and streamline the lead management process for dealerships of all sizes.

The Challenge of Fragmented Communication

In the digital age, customers reach out through an ever-growing array of channels, from traditional emails to messenger platforms and beyond. For many dealerships, managing these disparate sources of communication can be overwhelming, leading to delayed responses and lost sales. The Enquiry Hub addresses this challenge head-on, offering a unified solution that brings all customer interactions into one, easy-to-manage platform.

Key Features of the Enquiry Hub

  • Unified Inbox: Say goodbye to toggling between different applications and platforms. The Enquiry Hub consolidates all customer communications into a single, streamlined interface, ensuring no message ever gets overlooked.


  • Push Notifications: Stay on top of new inquiries with real-time alerts. Whether you're on the sales floor or on the move, our push notification system ensures you're always informed and ready to engage with potential buyers.


  • Team Collaboration: The Enquiry Hub isn't just about communication; it's about collaboration. In addition to our built-in chat functionality, your sales team can assign and create tasks and coordinate responses directly within the platform, enhancing teamwork and efficiency.

Why the Enquiry Hub Is a Game-Changer

The Enquiry Hub is more than just a tool; it's a strategic advantage. By shortening first response times, dealers can significantly increase their conversion rates. Studies show that responding within the first minute can boost conversions by up to 391%. Moreover, with consumer expectations leaning towards immediate engagement, the ability to provide quick responses is more crucial than ever.
