Reach and Convert In-Market Car Buyers

What are Automotive Vehicle Inventory Ads?

Automotive Inventory Ads (AIA) are dynamic advertisements that use your dealership's live vehicle inventory to create targeted ads. These ads are designed to reach in-market car buyers on various digital platforms, showcasing your real-time stock to potential customers.


How Does This Work?

By integrating your vehicle inventory feed into our system. We handle the setup, catalogue maintenance, and real-time updates, ensuring your ads always reflect your current inventory. These ads are then displayed on multiple platforms such as Google, Meta (Facebook & Instagram), TikTok, SnapChat, and Pinterest, targeting users based on their search behaviour and online activity.



Why are Automotive Inventory Ads effective?

Automotive Inventory Ads are effective because they target users who are actively looking for vehicles. By using data-driven targeting, these ads reach potential buyers who are in the market, increasing the likelihood of converting ad views into sales. Personalised ads can increase conversion rates by up to 10 times compared to non-targeted ads.

What platforms can I use for Automotive Inventory Ads?

Google, Meta (Facebook & Instagram), TikTok, SnapChat, and Pinterest. Each platform has its own unique audience and format requirements, which we manage to ensure optimal performance.

How does your solution simplify the advertising process?

Our solution simplifies the advertising process by handling all technical aspects, including feed setup, catalogue maintenance, event tracking, and campaign management. With our platform, you can preview ads, set budgets, and schedule campaigns from one portal, streamlining the entire process.

What is involved in setting up Automotive Inventory Ads myself?

Setting up Automotive Inventory Ads yourself involves creating and maintaining a real-time stock feed for multiple platforms, managing feed updates, setting up accurate event tracking, and logging into each platform to manage campaigns individually. This process is complex and time-consuming, requiring detailed technical knowledge.

How do Automotive Inventory Ads target in-market car buyers?

Automotive Inventory Ads target in-market car buyers by using data from users' search history, online behaviour, and demographic information. This precise targeting ensures that your ads reach users who are actively searching for vehicles, increasing the chances of conversion.


Can I track the performance of my Automotive Inventory Ads?

Yes, our platform includes comprehensive tracking and reporting tools. You can monitor the performance of your ads, including impressions, clicks, and conversions, to ensure your campaigns are achieving the desired results.
